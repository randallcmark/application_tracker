# ADR 2026-04-15: AI Is Embedded, Optional, And Inspectable

## Status

Accepted

## Decision

AI features are embedded guidance where work happens, not a hidden automation layer or separate assistant product.

## Rationale

- Recommendations, fit summaries, artefact suggestions, drafts, and profile observations are most useful in context.
- AI output must be stored as visible records or drafts users can review, edit, dismiss, or act on.
- The core tracker must continue to work without configured AI providers.
